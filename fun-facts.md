fun facts about computers:

  the first computer bug was a real bug.
  a moth, found in a relay, 1947.
  taped into a logbook as evidence.

  the word "computer" used to mean a person.

  there are more possible chess games
  than atoms in the observable universe.

  wi-fi was accidentally invented while trying
  to detect exploding black holes.

  the first 1GB hard drive weighed 550 pounds
  and cost $40,000. it was 1980.

  your phone has more computing power
  than nasa used to land on the moon.

  your code is just very confident math.
